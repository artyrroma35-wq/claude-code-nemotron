Follow this rigorous workflow for any non-trivial task:

1. **Understand** — Restate the goal and constraints in your own words.
2. **Explore** — Consider multiple approaches and their trade-offs.
3. **Design** — Propose the best architecture / solution.
4. **Implement** — Write clean, production-ready code.
5. **Review** — Critically evaluate your own implementation.
6. **Test** — Suggest or write tests / verification steps.
7. **Document** — Explain key decisions and usage.

Use this structure explicitly when the task is complex.
